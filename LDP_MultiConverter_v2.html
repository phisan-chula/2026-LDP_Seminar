Input

Accepted formats: P0 lon lat, P0 lat lon, RTK1 48 E N, RTK1 UTM48 E N, BLDG1 LDP E N. Comments after # are ignored. Commas in numbers are OK.

Detection rule: if the second token is LDP, the row is LDP. If it is 47, 48, UTM47, or UTM48, the row is UTM. Numeric thousands commas such as 278,234.264 are preserved as one number; commas between fields are still accepted. Otherwise two numeric values are treated as WGS84 and the app detects lon/lat vs lat/lon by value range.
